Dallas College AI Club: Building Together, Learning Together
Our most recent AI Club meeting focused on setting a strong foundation for long-term growth, collaboration, and impactful projects. Below is a summary of key discussions, upcoming initiatives, and how you can get involved.

🚀 Project Direction: Learning First, Building Smart
As a club, we discussed the importance of focus and scalability. Rather than spreading ourselves too thin, we may concentrate on a smaller number of projects with a shared understanding that much of our early work will involve:

Learning together – exploring AI tools, frameworks, and best practices as a community
Creating a shared code base – a reusable foundation that allows us to launch new AI projects faster and more efficiently in the future

This approach sets us up not just for individual success, but for sustainable club-wide innovation.

🤖 Spotlight Project: AI Success Coach (Chatbot)
One of the exciting ideas discussed was the AI Success Coach, an AI-powered chatbot designed to support student success. This is envisioned as a longer-term project and a perfect candidate to leverage our shared code base.
The AI Success Coach represents the kind of real-world, resume-ready project that can benefit students across Dallas College while giving our members hands-on experience with applied AI.
